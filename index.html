I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Department of Informatics and Telematics of Harokopio University of Athens and a Researcher at the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering , National Technical University of Athens, advised by Prof. Konsantinos Tserpes. I am also a Research Associate to the Institute of Communication and Computer Systems. I received a BSc Degree in Computer Science in 2013 and an MSc Degree in Web Engineering in 2015, both from Harokopio University of Athens. In 2022, I completed my PhD in the area of Distributed Systems at the same department.

I am currently a member of the f-coda research group, specializing in Efficient Computing and Data Analytics. My main research interests include Distributed Computing, Edge and Cloud Computing, Big Data Management and Analysis, Machine/Deep learning, NoSQL Database System, High Performance Computing (HPC), Spatiotemporal and Trajectory Analysis and Stream Processing.

I have participated in numerous EU funded and national projects.

Research Projects

↪ Ongoing Projects

  • PANDORA (HE)

    A Comprehensive Framework enabling the Delivery of Trustworthy Datasets for Efficient AIoT Operation.

  • CHARITY (H2020)

    CHARITY aspires to leverage the benefits of intelligent, autonomous orchestration of cloud, edge, and network resources, to create a symbiotic relationship between low and high latency infrastructures that will facilitate the needs of emerging applications.

  • SmartShip (H2020-MSCA-RISE-2018)

    A data analytics, decision support and circular economy – based multi-layer optimization platform towards a holistic energy efficiency, fuel consumption and emissions management of vessels.

↪ Past Projects

  • FNS-Cloud (H2020)

    Food Nutrition Security Cloud (FNS-Cloud) has launched the first ‘food cloud’, federating existing and emerging datasets, tools and services to support European research.

  • GLASSEAS (National funded ESPA 2019)

    A global, distributed surveillance system for early detection and analysis of anomalous vessel trajectories.

  • MASTER (H2020-MSCA-RISE-2017)

    MASTER (Multiple ASpects TrajEctoRy management and analysis) is a project with the objective of forming an international and inter-sectoral network of organisations working on a joint research programme to define new methods to build, manage and analyse multiple aspects semantic trajectories.

  • Productive 4.0 (H2020)

    Productive4.0 is an ambitious holistic innovation project, meant to open the doors to the potentials of Digital Industry and to maintain a leadership position of the industries in Europe. All partners involved will work on creating the capability to efficiently design and integrate hardware and software of Internet of Things (IoT) devices.

  • Optimal (HUA internally-funded project)

    Middleware development for the optimal replica placement in distributed key-value stores & fault tolerance techniques in infrastructures for large-scale data management.

  • Asylum Service Application (National funded)

    Mobile Application for international applicants protection.

  • Fortissimo (FP7)

    Cloud-based simulation of the binding capacities of target drug.
